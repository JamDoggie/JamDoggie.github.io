A GMod Gamemode where you fling yourself at other people to hurt them!

With semi-competitive and semi-casual ragdoll based gameplay, verse your friends in either Free for All or Teams mode. On any supported maps, you may even find weapon drops laying on the ground throughout the round. The weapons are as follows:


- Shotgun, has two bullets and a spread. You can choose to either use one bullet at a time or two with right click.

- 357, has one bullet and does tons of damage, but can be very hard to hit with!

- Crossbow, a hitscan/non projectile based weapon that sends whoever it hits flying into ragdoll mode as well as doing 25 damage.

- Ricochet Disk, a disc that you can throw in the horizontal axis that ricochets off walls and does 50 damage to whoever it hits that isn't you or your teammates.

- Throwable Crowbar, a crowbar that you can throw that will usually one shot enemies but is very hard to hit.
